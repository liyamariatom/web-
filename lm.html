Ponkunnam to Chengalam

Explore the beauty of Ponkunnam and Chengalam through their iconic spots and scenic views.

Ponkunnam

Ponkunnam, a bustling town in Kerala, is renowned for its cultural heritage, markets, and spiritual sites.

Puthiyakavu Devi Temple

This temple is a symbol of spiritual serenity, with intricate carvings and vibrant festivals that attract visitors year-round.

Ponkunnam Market

The market is a lively place filled with fresh produce, local spices, and handmade crafts. It's a true cultural experience.

Cultural Events

Ponkunnam hosts annual cultural events showcasing traditional Kerala music, dance, and art forms.

Chengalam

Chengalam is a serene village known for its lush greenery, scenic landscapes, and tranquil spots.

Nature Trails

The village offers beautiful nature trails that are perfect for hiking and bird watching.

St Antony's Chruch

The Chengalam market is a small but charming place where locals sell organic produce and handmade goods.

Festivals and Celebrations

Chengalam's festivals are vibrant and filled with traditional music, dance, and colorful decorations.
